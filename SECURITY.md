# Security Policy

## Sensitive data

This project intentionally avoids handling secrets in ways that are easy to leak.

- Do not paste cookies into issues, PRs, logs, or screenshots.
- Prefer `--cookie-file` over `--cookie`-style CLI arguments.
- Never commit cookies, exports, or personal data to git.

## Responsible usage

You are responsible for ensuring you have permission to export content and that your usage complies with applicable laws and the target service terms.
